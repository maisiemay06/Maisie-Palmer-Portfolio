After almost three years merchandising for a fast fashion supplier, I decided to leave the industry in pursuit of a career that better suits my abilities, aspirations and personal strengths. I began learning to code in my spare time and switched to a full time training course shortly after when I realised that a future in front-end development was an ideal focus for me. I am now looking for the opportunity to join a cohesive team that I can bring my own perspective to while being supported in my learning and development.
I am hard-working and creative with excellent organisational skills and a dedication to learn and grow. I am calm and collected, aim to lead by example in all situations and am able to work proficiently in both group settings and as an individual. What I lack in industry experience, I make up for in drive and ambition to develop my skills, and become part of a successful developer team.
